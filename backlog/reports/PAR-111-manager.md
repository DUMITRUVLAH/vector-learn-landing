# PAR-111 Persona: Andreea Mitran (Director)

**Verdict: BUY**

"Notifications are what make this PAR workflow actually functional in practice. Without them, requests just sit silently in the system and people forget to check.

The events covered are exactly right:
- Submit → approver gets notified immediately
- Each approval step → next approver gets notified 
- Final approval → finance knows to act
- Reject/changes → requestor isn't left hanging

The fact that it writes to our existing in-app notification center (the one we already use for mentions and system alerts) means people don't need to check multiple places. One bell icon, all alerts.

The email fallback is useful for approvers who don't open the app daily.

One note: I'd like to eventually see quiet hours for email (not at midnight), but that's a preference setting (SET-8xx scope), not a blocker for now."

**Friction for future:** quiet hours/preference settings for PAR email notifications (SET module scope).
