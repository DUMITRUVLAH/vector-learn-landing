# COURSE-202 — Persona Manager Report (Andreea Mitran)

**Verdict: BUY**

## What I reviewed
Group capacity management with max_students enforcement, waitlist, and capacity badge.

## What works
- "6/8" capacity badge is instantly clear — no need to count manually
- When a class is full, system automatically puts students on waitlist (no confusion)
- First student on waitlist is auto-promoted when someone drops — saves me manual follow-up
- FIFO ordering is fair and I can explain it to parents ("you registered at 10:05, someone else at 10:00 was first")

## Quote
"Sala 3 are 8 scaune. Înainte pierdeam timp să ne uităm în foi câți sunt înrolați. Acum sistemul îmi spune 6/8 și știu exact."

## Friction
- Would want to see the waitlist names (not just count) so I can call them when a spot opens
- Future: email notification when promoted from waitlist

## Verdict explanation
Solves a real daily operational pain. Overbooking creates complaints and chaos. Capacity cap + auto-promotion is the correct pattern.
