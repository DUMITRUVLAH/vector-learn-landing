# COURSE-202 — Persona Student Report (Maria + Cristina)

**Verdict: OK**

## Maria (14yo student)
This is mostly an admin tool, not student-facing. But if I try to join a class that's full, I'd rather get a clear "you're on the waitlist" message than no message at all. The "full" indicator is honest.

## Cristina (Maria's mom)
The waitlist feature means I can register my daughter for a class even when it's full and trust she'll be moved up automatically if someone drops. I don't have to call to check every week. The "6/8" badge tells the receptionist exactly what's available so there's no miscommunication.

## Quote (Cristina)
"M-a sunat doamna de la recepție să-mi zică că Maria a intrat în clasă — cineva a anulat și locul ei a trecut automat. Bine că nu mai trebuia eu să sun în fiecare zi."

## Friction
- Neither of us can see the waitlist directly from a student/parent portal (future feature)

## Verdict explanation
OK — indirect benefit for students/parents. The friction is in the missing parent-facing transparency (future).
